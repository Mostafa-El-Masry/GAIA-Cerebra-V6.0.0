This lesson is not about fixing yourself.
It is about stopping the damage.

Self-healing, in this system, means learning how to remain functional while carrying pressure, uncertainty, and unfinished stories.

## What Self-Healing Is
- Creating space between stimulus and reaction
- Reducing internal noise
- Learning to pause without collapsing
- Staying present without forcing optimism

## What Self-Healing Is NOT
- Positive thinking
- Motivation
- Productivity
- "Becoming better" overnight

## The Core Skill
The skill you are building is **awareness without judgment**.

When something feels heavy:
- You do not fix it immediately
- You do not analyze it endlessly
- You notice it and keep moving

## Why This Exists in Academy
Because learning, money, and systems fail when the internal state is unstable.

This path exists to:
- Prevent burnout
- Reduce self-sabotage
- Keep progress slow but intact
